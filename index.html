Causes Of Cardiovascualr Disease

Cardiovascular disease is caused by damage to all or part of the Cardiovascular, damage to the coronary arteries, or a poor supply of nutrients and oxygen to the organ. There are a number of lifestyle choices that can increase the risk of Cardiovascular disease. These include:

  • High blood pressure and cholestero
  • Smoking
  • Overweight and obesity
  • Diabetes
  • Family history
  • A diet of junk food
  • Age Read More

Symptoms of cardiovascular disease

The symptoms of Cardiovascular disease depend on which condition is affecting an individual.However, common symptoms include:

  • Chest pain
  • Breathlessness
  • Cardiovascular palpitations Read More

Strategies to prevent Cardiovascular disease

  • Don't smoke or use tobacco
  • Get moving: Aim for at least 30 to 60 minutes of activity daily
  • Eat a Cardiovascular-healthy diet
  • Maintain a healthy weight
  • Get good quality sleep
  • Manage stress
  • Get regular health screeningsRead More
